Founder & First Director, Pita Taylor-Heke.

About the Founder:

He is a young Māori professional and “digital creative” (Toi ā Hangarau), well versed in a range of creative and technical pursuits. He studied at Victoria University of Wellington,
(known as Vic Uni to those who attend), gaining a Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce (Majors: Economics, Geography).
He also studied a Design Degree (Design Innovation) majoring in Media Design, which improved his skills in
Animation, Game Development, Web Design and general design philosophy.

  • 25+ years musician – guitar (bass, acoustic, electric), drums, keyboard and many more
  • 25+ years artist – portraits, water colour, charcoal (fine arts), comic/anime style and so forth
  • 15+ years web development (HTML, CSS, Javascript)
  • 10+ years programming (Java, Javascript, Python, C#, C++)
  • 10+ years game development - demos, prototypes and small scale experiences
  • 10+ years 2D animation - hand drawn and digital
  • 4+ years music production – small jingles for games and videos
  • 4+ years video production – small form videos, promo videos for company
  • 2+ years 3D modelling


Professional Work:
  • Ministry for Culture & Heritage | Manatū Taonga – Gaming Advisor
  • Wild Eye Interactive – Development & Research (Games)
  • Kiwi Game’s Starter – Game Judge (Volunteer)
