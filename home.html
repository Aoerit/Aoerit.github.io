Aoerit [pronounced "eə(r)rɪt" or "Aer-rit"] is a games and production company, based in the "River City" of Whanganui, NZ.

Māori owned and operated, Aoerit is focused on creating, developing and producing "digital experiences" via Mobile ,PC and Mixed Reality, AR and VR) platforms .

Digital Experiences: including educational elements, kaupapa and tīkanga Māori (protocols and customs of Māori).
all while being fun to play!

About the Founder:

A young Māori professional and “digital creative” (Toi ā Hangarau), well versed in a range of creative and technical pursuits. Studied at Victoria University of Wellington, gaining a Bachelor’s Degree in Commerce (Majors: Economics, Geography).
Also studied a Design Degree majoring in Media Design – Animation, Game Development, Web Design.

  • 25+ years musician – guitar (bass, acoustic, electric), drums, keyboard and many more
  • 25+ years artist – portraits, water colour, charcoal (fine arts), comic/anime style and so forth
  • 15+ years web development (HTML, CSS, Javascript)
  • 10+ years programming (Java, Javascript, Python, C#, C++)
  • 10+ years game development - demos, prototypes and small scale experiences
  • 10+ years 2D animation - hand drawn and digital
  • 4+ years music production – small jingles for games and videos
  • 4+ years video production – small form videos, promo videos for company
