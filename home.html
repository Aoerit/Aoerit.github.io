Aoerit [pronounced "eə(r)rɪt" or "Aer-rit"] is a games and production company, based in the "River City" of Whanganui, NZ.

Māori owned and operated, Aoerit is focused on creating, developing and producing digital experiences (games) via Mobile, PC and Mixed Reality (AR and VR) platforms.

Digital Experiences: including educational elements, kaupapa and tīkanga Māori
