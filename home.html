Aoerit [pronounced "eə(r)rɪt" or "Aer-rit"] is a games and production company, based in the "River City" of Whanganui, NZ.

Māori owned and operated, the company is primarily focused on creating digital experiences (games) via Mobile, PC and VR platforms.
