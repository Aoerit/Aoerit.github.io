Aoerit [pronounced "eə(r)rɪt" or "Aer-rit"] is a games and production company, based in the "River City" of Whanganui, New Zealand.

Māori owned and operated, Aoerit Ltd. is focused on creating, developing and producing "digital experiences" via Mobile , PC and Mixed Reality, (Augemnted Reality and Virtual Reality) platforms. .

Digital Experiences: including educational elements, kaupapa and tīkanga Māori (protocols and customs of Māori)
- all while being fun to play!
